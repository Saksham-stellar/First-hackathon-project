🤖 About SmartKrishi

SmartKrishi is an AI-based crop recommendation system designed to help farmers choose the most suitable crop for their soil and climate conditions. Our model uses rule-based scoring with explainability — meaning it compares soil nutrient values, pH, rainfall, and temperature against ideal crop ranges and assigns scores. This ensures transparency and trust, so farmers understand why a crop is recommended.

🌟 Why Our Model?

  • Uses soil nutrient data (N, P, K, pH), climate, and rainfall inputs.
  • Calculates suitability scores for multiple crops.
  • Explains the reasoning — not just “black box” predictions.
  • Visualizes data with radar, bar, and doughnut charts for easy interpretation.
